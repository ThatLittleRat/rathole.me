📜 My Story

It all started with an eraser.

One day in school, I was crawling under my classmate’s desk trying to find it. She looked down, saw me under there, and screamed in laughter:

“Patkány!!” (which means rat in Hungarian)

From that moment, the name stuck. Everyone started calling me Rat — and instead of running from it, I owned it. That's how ThatLittleRat was born.
